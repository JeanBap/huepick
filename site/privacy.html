HuePick Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026

HuePick is built to be private by default. This policy explains exactly what it does and does not do with your data.

What we collect

Nothing. HuePick does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no trackers, and no remote servers.

What is stored, and where

The colours you pick are saved as a palette using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. This data lives only on your device and is never sent anywhere. You can clear it any time with the "clear" button in the popup, or by removing the extension.

Permissions

HuePick requests a single permission, storage, used solely to persist your palette locally. It does not read page content, browsing history, or any other data.
