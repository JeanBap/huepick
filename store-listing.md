# Chrome Web Store listing — HuePick (v2.0.0)

**Name:** HuePick — Color Picker, Palettes & Page Scanner
**Summary:** Eyedrop colours, scan a whole page's palette, manage named palettes, export to CSS/Tailwind, and check WCAG contrast & colour-blindness. Local-first, no tracking.
**Category:** Developer Tools
**Single purpose:** Pick, collect, analyse and export colours from the screen and web pages.
**Permission justification:**
- storage — saves your palettes locally on your device.
- scripting + activeTab — used only when you click Pick or Scan, to read a colour or the page's colours from the current tab. No background access.
**Data usage:** Collects/transmits nothing. Palettes stored only in chrome.storage.local.
**Privacy policy URL:** https://huepick.yanni.uk/privacy.html
