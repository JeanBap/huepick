HuePick Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026 · v2.0.0

HuePick is private by default. Here is exactly what it does and does not do.

What we collect

Nothing. No analytics, no trackers, no remote servers. HuePick never transmits, sells or shares any data.

What is stored, and where

Your palettes are saved with Chrome's chrome.storage.local on your device only. Clear them any time with the delete button or by removing the extension.

Pick and Scan

When you click Pick, HuePick reads the single colour you select. When you click Scan, it reads the colours used on the page you are viewing, in memory, only to show them to you. Neither action stores or sends page content anywhere.

Permissions

storage saves palettes locally. scripting and activeTab are used only at the moment you click Pick or Scan, on the current tab. There is no background access to your browsing.
